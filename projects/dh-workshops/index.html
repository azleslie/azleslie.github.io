Digital Humanities Workshops

Feb 04, 2020

Since the Spring 2018 semester I have served as the Digital Humanities Graduate Specialist for Rutgers Libraries. Over the course of that time I’ve developed a number of workshops, from basic tutorials on using software popular among digital humanists (Gephi, Paladio, Tropy, etc.) to some introductory and even intermediate lessons on R programming in the humanities.

Most of this material - code, printouts, and sample data - can be found on my GitHub page.. I list them here so that they don’t get buried there under future repositories.
