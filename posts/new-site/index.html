New Site

Feb 04, 2020

I decided to bite the bullet and make a website. I hope you like it.

You’ll find various professional materials collected here, complete with links and downloads. I also hope to use this site as a place to make available some of the datasets from my research and to share the occasional thought on materials or methods.

I wanted something that was minimalist in both aesthetics and organization so that I could keep the content local, get under the hood, and get a bit more experience with HTML, CSS, and the command line along the way. I decided on the open-source static site generator Hugo, GitHub Pages hosting, and the theme Manis, with some tweaks of my own.

Having experimented with a few other options first (and only really broken things once) I’m quite pleased with how this setup works. I’ll probably have more to say about it in either blog post or workshop format in the next couple weeks.

Made with Hugo and Manis
