New Site

Feb 04, 2020

I decided to bite the bullet and make a website.

You’ll find various professional materials collected here, complete with links and downloads. I also hope, however, to use this as a place to make available some of the datasets from my research.

I used the open-source static site generator Hugo, GitHub Pages hosting, and the theme Manis, with some minor tweaks of my own. I wanted something that was minimalist in both aesthetics and organization so that I could keep the content local, get under the hood, and learn more HTML and CSS along the way. Having experimented with a few other options first, I’m pleased with how this setup works. I’ll probably say more about this in the next couple weeks.
